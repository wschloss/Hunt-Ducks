Duck Hunt!

About Credits

Duck Hunt is a classic! Shoot down ducks and enjoy the old school vibe without having to deal with that judgmental dog! Point and click to shoot, and see how many ducks you can handle before they fly off. Each level adds a duck and decreases your time to get them all.
